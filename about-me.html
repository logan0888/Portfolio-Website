About Me

I’m Logan, a junior in high school, a professional front-end web developer in training, and an all-around tech enthusiast from south Georgia. I have explored server management and deployment, and have now found my true passion in front-end web design and development. I have worked with Sunbury Charters LLC, a fishing charter in Sunbury, Georgia, to develop and self-host a business website for them. The working product is now in full production and can be found at https://sunburyfishingcharter.com . I’m always looking for new, challenging projects to work on, so if you have something, don't hesitate to reach out! When I’m not working on improving my skills or working on projects, I usually spend my time hanging out with friends, playing video games, and working on my car. I am also currently involved in the Boy Scouts of America and am working towards earning my Eagle Scout rank. Stay up to date with what I’m doing by going to the Stay Connected page!
